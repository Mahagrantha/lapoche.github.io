Our story

Made with roots,
worn with purpose

La Poche was born from a simple idea — that the things we carry every day should be beautiful, durable, and kind to the world we live in. We partnered with master weavers in rural India to bring centuries-old jute craftsmanship into the modern wardrobe.

Every bag tells a story of hands at work, of natural fibres pulled from the earth, woven into something that lasts. No plastic. No shortcuts. Just jute.
