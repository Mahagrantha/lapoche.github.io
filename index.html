Our Story

A bag
made with
intention.

La Poche began in 2019 with a single loom in Murshidabad and a belief that everyday objects deserve to be made beautifully. We work directly with artisan clusters across West Bengal, paying fair wages and keeping batches small.

Every bag is woven by hand, QC'd by eye, and shipped in packaging that breaks down in your compost bin. No middlemen, no mass production.

🧺
🌿
Sustainably Sourced
Our jute comes from certified farms in West Bengal. It grows with minimal water, no pesticides, and sequesters carbon as it grows.
🤝
Fairly Made
We pay above-market wages to our 24 artisans, provide health benefits, and reinvest 5% of revenue into community skill-building.
♻️
Zero Plastic
All our packaging — bags, mailers, tissue, tape — is either recycled, recyclable, or compostable. Nothing enters the conversation in plastic.
